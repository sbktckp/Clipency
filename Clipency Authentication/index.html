FAQ

Common Questions

How do clippers get paid?
+
Clippers earn a per-view rate for every verified view their clips generate across all platforms. Payments are processed on a 30-day rolling cycle directly to your bank or UPI. No minimum payout threshold for your first withdrawal.
What content can I clip?
+
We provide a licensed content library from our brand partners. You never need to source content yourself — just pick from our curated pool, create your clip, and publish. All content is pre-cleared for distribution rights.
How quickly can a brand go live?
+
Most brand campaigns go live within 72 hours of onboarding. Our team handles clipper briefing, content guidelines, and platform setup. You just provide your raw footage or brand assets.
Is there a minimum spend for brands?
+
We have a minimum campaign threshold to ensure quality distribution across our network. Reach out to our team for custom pricing — we work with early-stage startups to enterprise brands alike.
Which platforms does Clipency distribute to?
+
Currently: TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. We're expanding to Snapchat Spotlight and LinkedIn video in Q2 2025. Clippers can publish to any or all platforms from a single dashboard.
Do I need an existing audience to be a clipper?
+
No. Many of our highest earners started with zero followers. The algorithm rewards well-edited, engaging clips — not follower count. We provide editing tips and performance feedback to help you grow.

WHAT CLIPENCY IS

Built for campaigns that need to move.

The work is not just posting. It is briefing, distributing, proving, reviewing and paying. Clipency brings those steps into one place so campaigns feel less scattered and more accountable.

01

Campaigns become structured

Every campaign can carry its goal, platforms, category, payout logic, submission rules and review status. The result is a clean distribution brief that creators can act on.

02

Creators become operators

Clippers discover campaigns, publish content, submit proof links and track their performance inside one creator workspace.

03

Proof becomes visible

Submissions, views, status, notes and approvals become traceable instead of being buried in screenshots and chats.

04

Teams get operating clarity

Clipency is designed to reduce manual coordination and make creator-led campaigns easier to review, approve, repeat and scale.
